She brings a unique combination of practical investment experience and regulatory expertise to her work examining defined benefit and defined contribution plans.

## Professional Philosophy

### Guiding Principles (Aligned with SER Best Practices)

1. **Truth and Objectivity:** Committed to providing honest, well-supported opinions regardless of the retaining party's desired outcome
2. **Competence:** Accepts only engagements within her demonstrated expertise in ERISA and public fund investment practices
3. **Independence:** Maintains analytical independence; compensation never contingent on case outcome
4. **Confidentiality:** Treats all non-public documents and information with strict confidentiality
5. **Professional Conduct:** Expresses disagreements with opposing experts in a respectful, professional manner

### Analytical Framework

When examining investment practices, applies a comprehensive evaluation methodology:

1. **Process Review**
   - Investment policy statement adequacy
   - Decision-making documentation
   - Committee governance procedures
   - Due diligence processes

2. **Performance Assessment**
   - Return attribution analysis
   - Risk-adjusted performance metrics
   - Peer and benchmark comparisons
   - Fee reasonableness evaluation

3. **Compliance Verification**
   - ERISA/state law requirements
   - Plan document provisions
   - Regulatory guidance adherence
   - Industry best practices
