## Professional Summary

MK Campion is the Chief Investment Officer (CIO) and Director of Institutional Portfolio Management at Avior Wealth Management. She is responsible for client and strategy investment policies, private and public manager research and due diligence, and trading activities. Campion is a member of the Securities Expert Roundtable (“SERs”) and the Public Investors Advocacy Bar Association (“PIABA”). Prior to joining Avior, Campion was president and founder of Champion Capital Research, Inc. She has decades of experience as a financial economist and portfolio strategist and is considered an expert in fiduciary excellence and the design of asset allocation strategies for institutional investment portfolios. Campion conducts investment practice and performance examinations of defined benefit and contribution plans to ensure investment policies and practices of public funds are consistent with ERISA and state laws and regulations. Campion is published in academic and professional journals including *Bank Asset/Liability Management*, *The North America Journal of Economics and Finance*, *The World Economy: The Americas*, and *The TEXPERS Pension Observer*. She is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA®) and an Accredited Investment Fiduciary Auditor (AIFA®). Dr. Campion holds Bachelor, Masters, and Ph.D. degrees from Rice University and the University of Houston.
